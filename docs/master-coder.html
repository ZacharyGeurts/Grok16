Unified driver (16.1.0)

One binary: $G16_PREFIX/bin/g16. Backends: libexec/grok16/g16-cc, libexec/grok16/g16-cxx. g++16 → symlink to g16. CMake sets both CMAKE_C_COMPILER and CMAKE_CXX_COMPILER to g16.
