2D field platform

Grok16 5.1.0 — flat operator plane. Everything on the plane is auto-field at depth 0.

DO NOT CREATE FIELD FILES. Standalone .field, depth-field, or subfield JSON heats neighboring fields on the fabric. Gates flatten stray depth, but thermo debt hits adjacent dies first. Place entities on the 2D platform instead.
