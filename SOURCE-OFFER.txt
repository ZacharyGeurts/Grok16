Grok16 — Written Offer for Corresponding Source (GPL v3 §6b)
=============================================================

This offer applies to object-code distributions of the Grok16 compiler
toolchain (g16, g++16, g16-as, g16-ld, and related binutils/gcc binaries)
built from GNU Compiler Collection and GNU Binutils sources.

Corresponding Source
--------------------

1. Upstream GCC (branch releases/gcc-15):
   https://gcc.gnu.org/git/gcc.git

2. Upstream GNU Binutils (release used at bootstrap):
   https://sourceware.org/git/binutils-gdb.git

3. Grok16 modifications (patches, forge, driver, scripts, cmake):
   https://github.com/ZacharyGeurts/Grok16

   Documented field deltas:
   - patches/gcc-base-ver-16.0.0.patch (BASE-VER 15.3.1 → 16.0.0)
   - forge bootstrap configures --program-transform-name for g16/g++16
   - driver/g16-unified.c (original Grok16 dispatch driver)

4. Rebuild from source on this machine:
   ./scripts/grok16-toolchain.sh bootstrap
   ./scripts/grok16-toolchain.sh verify

Written offer
-------------

For at least three years from the date you received these binaries, we offer
to provide the Corresponding Source on a durable physical medium for a charge
no more than our reasonable cost of physically performing source conveyance.
